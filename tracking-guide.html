Email Notifications

We'll send you email updates at each stage of your order:

  • Order Confirmation: Immediate confirmation with order details
  • Processing: When your order is being prepared
  • Shipped: When your order leaves our warehouse
  • Out for Delivery: When your order is out for final delivery
  • Delivered: Confirmation of successful delivery

Make sure to check your spam folder if you don't receive emails.
